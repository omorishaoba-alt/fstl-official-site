Problem Statement

FHA and FMBN’s overlapping roles, weak capitalization and absence of effective secondary market instruments have constrained mortgage penetration and slowed affordable housing delivery.

FSTL Recommended Reforms

  • Segregate mandates: FHA – focus on social & affordable housing; FMBN – become a true secondary liquidity engine.
  • Securitisation framework: Create legal scaffolding for MBS/ABS issuance, servicer standards and investor protections.
  • Title & Mortgage Insurance: Mandate title verification and mortgage insurance as preconditions for mortgage purchase by the secondary market.
  • Municipal financing: Empower states/municipalities to issue asset-backed bonds for local infrastructure & housing.

Expected Outcomes

  • Increased mortgage liquidity and longer tenors
  • Greater private sector participation in housing supply
  • Reduced dependence on foreign borrowing for local infrastructure
