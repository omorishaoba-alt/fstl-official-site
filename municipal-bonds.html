Why Municipal Bonds

Municipal bonds raise Naira funding locally for projects such as roads, water and housing — avoiding FX risk and mobilizing domestic pension and insurance capital.

FSTL Role

  • Title verification and asset readiness for bond-backed projects
  • Structuring guarantees & risk reports to attract institutional investors
  • Advisory on revenue streams, covenants and project monitoring

Expected Benefits

  • Lower dependence on foreign debt
  • Improved municipal governance and project accountability
  • Creation of long-dated Naira instruments for pension funds
