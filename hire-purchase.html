Overview

Hire-purchase credit provides structured installment-based financing for property acquisition, enabling access for individuals and developers who may not have full upfront capital.

Key Benefits

  • Increases home ownership and property investment participation.
  • Encourages responsible repayment behavior, supporting a strong credit culture.
  • Allows developers to secure advance payments for housing projects.
  • Integrates with title and mortgage insurance to reduce lender risk.

FSTL Implementation

  • Designs hire-purchase programs backed by verified title guarantees.
  • Partners with banks and microfinance institutions to expand access.
  • Supports documentation, legal compliance, and enforcement of contracts.

Statistics

  • Less than 10% of Nigerian households access formal hire-purchase financing (NBS, 2025).
  • Properly structured hire-purchase schemes reduce default rates by 70%.
  • Expands affordable housing access, particularly in secondary cities and peri-urban areas.
