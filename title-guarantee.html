Title Guarantee (Primary)

Product Overview: FSTL’s Primary Title Guarantee ensures that buyers, lenders, and investors can transact with full confidence on properly documented and registered land titles.

  • Coverage: Fully registered and verified titles.
  • Eligibility: Any property with clear title documentation.
  • Benefits:
    • Legal and financial indemnity in case of title defects.
    • Fraud and forgery protection.
    • Enables financing and mortgage access with confidence.
    • Increases marketability and value of the property.
  • Activation: Purchase via secure FSTL Payment Methods.

The Primary Title Guarantee is the cornerstone of FSTL’s title protection and land securitisation framework.

Payment Methods

  • Bank Transfer – Direct deposit into FSTL’s verified accounts.
  • Mobile Money – Pay via M-Pesa, Paga, or Flutterwave.
  • Online Gateway – Cards, USSD, or QR code.
  • Cheque / Bank Draft – For corporate/bulk payments.
  • Standing Order / Subscription – For recurring product packages.
