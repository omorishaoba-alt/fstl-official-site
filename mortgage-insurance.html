Overview

Mortgage Insurance reduces lender and investor risk by protecting against borrower default, facilitating the flow of credit and enabling affordable housing access.

Why Mortgage Insurance is Critical in Nigeria

  • Nigeria faces a housing deficit exceeding 17 million units (NBS, 2025)
  • Traditional lenders face high default risk without insurance
  • Mortgage insurance creates confidence for banks to extend credit
  • Supports development of a robust mortgage market and secondary market liquidity

FSTL’s Approach

  1. Risk assessment for individual borrowers and corporate investors
  2. Integration with Title Guarantee & Hire-Purchase Credit
  3. Premiums structured to maintain affordability while covering risk
  4. Reporting and verification through blockchain cadastral records

Benefits to Stakeholders

  • Borrowers gain access to affordable mortgage finance
  • Lenders can confidently issue larger volumes of loans
  • Government institutions can incentivize housing finance
  • Investors gain confidence in securitized mortgage-backed instruments

Integration with FSTL Ecosystem

Mortgage Insurance works hand-in-hand with:

  • Title Guarantee – ensures the property is legally valid
  • Hire-Purchase Credit – affordable structured payments
  • Municipal Bonds & Infrastructure Bonds – financing the development pipeline
  • Cadastral & Blockchain verification – secure, immutable property data
