Overview

Mortgage insurance mitigates risk for lenders in the event of borrower default. FSTL has provided comprehensive mortgage insurance for over 20 years, enabling Nigerian banks, investors, and homeowners to engage in safe and reliable lending.

Key Benefits

  • Protects lenders against default risk, ensuring liquidity and stability in mortgage markets.
  • Encourages banks to extend credit to first-time homebuyers and low-income earners.
  • Supports secondary market initiatives and securitisation of mortgage portfolios.
  • Increases investor confidence in Nigeria’s real estate and housing finance market.

FSTL Approach

  • Rigorous borrower assessment and property verification.
  • Integration with title insurance and municipal bond financing programs.
  • Tailored coverage for diverse property types, including residential, commercial, and industrial estates.
  • Supports risk-based pricing for sustainable and scalable mortgage insurance schemes.

Impact on Nigeria’s Economy

  • Strengthens mortgage lending and credit culture.
  • Facilitates property development, urbanisation, and land industrialisation.
  • Increases housing affordability and access through structured finance.
  • Supports national strategies for housing deficit reduction.
