What is Title Guarantee?

A statement and product backed by due diligence and indemnity that gives lenders and buyers confidence the title is free of undisclosed claims and encumbrances.

Process

  1. Document verification (C of O, Gazette, survey plan)
  2. Encumbrance and litigation search
  3. Survey reconciliation & cadastral mapping
  4. Issue title grade and guarantee policy

Why It Matters

  • Makes properties eligible as collateral
  • Reduces loan losses and litigation exposure
  • Enables securitisation and municipal bond linkage
