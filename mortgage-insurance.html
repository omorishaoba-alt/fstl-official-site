Overview

Mortgage insurance protects lenders and investors against borrower default, enabling financial institutions to lend confidently to more households, including low-income earners.

Key Benefits

  • Reduces credit risk and enhances mortgage liquidity.
  • Encourages banks to offer longer-term, lower-interest mortgages.
  • Supports housing finance expansion and affordable housing development.
  • Creates a credit culture in Nigeria by formalizing risk coverage.

FSTL’s Role

  • Provides mortgage insurance backed by verified title guarantees.
  • Assesses property and borrower risk using our 20-year expertise.
  • Partners with banks to design scalable and secure mortgage products.
  • Integrates with FHA and FMBN reforms to promote secondary market liquidity.

Statistics

  • Less than 3% of Nigeria’s housing stock is financed through mortgages (FMBN, 2025).
  • Default risk remains a major barrier to mortgage expansion without insurance.
  • Mortgage insurance increases lending capacity by up to 40% for banks using FSTL services.
