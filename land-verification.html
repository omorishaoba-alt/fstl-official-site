Why Land Verification matters

We ensure that property is legally transferable, free of hidden encumbrances and suitable as collateral for lending or securitisation.

  • Title & registry searches (C of O, Gazette, Excision)
  • Survey plan authentication and field confirmation
  • Encumbrance & litigation checks
  • Ownership chain verification

Our process

  1. Document collection and registry cross-check.
  2. Field verification and survey alignment.
  3. Risk scoring and title integrity grading (A+ to D).
  4. Report and certificate for lenders/investors.
