Overview

FSTL’s Back-Up Title Guarantee safeguards properties that are imperfect, pending approval, or undergoing legal regularisation. It provides investors, buyers, and lenders with confidence even when full title perfection has not yet been achieved.

Key Coverage

  • Properties with incomplete or pending documentation
  • Governor’s Consent not yet issued
  • Encumbrance resolution and clean-up assistance
  • Legal and administrative regularisation support

Benefits

  • Allows transactions to proceed without unnecessary delays
  • Minimises exposure to title disputes
  • Facilitates financing even with imperfect titles
  • Enables phased property development while title is regularised

How It Works

  1. FSTL reviews existing documentation and identifies gaps
  2. Legal and regulatory assessment conducted for potential risks
  3. Back-Up Guarantee issued with coverage and remedies
  4. Continuous monitoring until full title perfection
