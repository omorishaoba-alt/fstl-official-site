Key statutes

  • Land Use Act 1978 — land allocation, occupancy and Governor’s consent.
  • Property & Conveyancing Law — assignments, leases and conveyancing formalities.
  • Registration of Titles Acts — registration, perfection and priority rules.
  • Evidence Act & Contract Law — admissibility of documents and contract validity.

Major cases

  • Ogunleye v Oni (1990) — transfer cannot exceed title.
  • Osho v Foreign Finance Corp (1991) — registered title priority.
  • Savannah Bank v Ajilo (1989) — Governor’s consent mandatory.
  • Adeniran v Alao (2002) — possession does not override documented title.
  • Idundun v Okumagba (1976) — recognized methods of proving title.
