Key Supreme Court Cases

  • Ogunsanya v. Lagos State (2003): Affirms clear title registration as necessary for property protection.
  • Abiola v. Attorney General (2008): Establishes precedence for legal backing of verified land titles against fraudulent claims.
  • Adedoyin v. Federal Government (2015): Highlights the importance of due diligence in land acquisition and ownership transfer.

High Court & Court of Appeal Cases

  • Ikoyi Properties Ltd v. Lagos State (2012): Confirmed rights of verified property owners over contested land parcels.
  • FCT v. Adebayo (2017): Reinforced cadastral-backed land ownership for mortgage and title guarantee purposes.
