What Is Land Verification?

Land Verification is the foundational property protection service offered by First Standard Title Limited (FSTL). It ensures that every buyer, investor, developer, and financial institution receives accurate, legally backed information about the true status of any land in Nigeria before committing funds.

Over 70% of property fraud cases can be prevented by proper verification. FSTL brings professionalism, legal backing, and transparency to the land acquisition process.

Why This Service Is Critical

  • Avoid purchasing government-acquired land.
  • Confirm if land is under litigation.
  • Verify ownership claims and confirm identity of sellers.
  • Check if land is committed to government projects.
  • Authenticate documents: survey, excision, gazette, C of O, and R of O.
  • Protect buyers from fake family representatives (“omo onile”).
  • Prevent double-selling and land encroachment issues.

What We Check (Full Due Diligence Scope)

  1. Verification at State Land Registry
  2. Survey Plan Authentication at Surveyor-General’s Office
  3. Cadastral Boundary Confirmation
  4. Ownership Identity Confirmation (Backed with Biometrics)
  5. Litigation & Encumbrance Search
  6. Government Acquisition Search
  7. Title Document Authentication (C of O, Gazette, Excision)
  8. Traditional/Family Ownership Investigation
  9. On-site Physical Inspection & Boundary Verification
  10. Neighbourhood Risk Assessment

Deliverables

You will receive:

  • Detailed Land Verification Report (LVR)
  • Risk Rating Score (LTRR)

  • Eligibility Status for Title Guarantee (if applying)
