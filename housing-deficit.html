Scale

Nigeria’s housing deficit estimates vary; official and industry estimates range between 17–30 million units depending on methodology (urban backlog, household formation rates and replacement needs).

Drivers

  • Rapid urbanisation and population growth
  • Insufficient formal mortgage supply
  • Land and title insecurity
  • High construction costs and weak supply chain

FSTL Interventions

  • Title verification to unlock bank financing
  • Integration with hire-purchase and mortgage insurance
  • Advising municipal bond-funded housing projects
