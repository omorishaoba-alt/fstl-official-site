Executive Overview

Nigeria is capital-deficient, not poor. FSTL has over 20 years of experience in land, title risk, mortgage risk, and property-based financing. NATICON provides a structured framework to convert land and property into bankable capital.

  • Affordable housing finance and mortgages
  • Bankable land and property assets
  • Profitable public-private participation
  • Reduction of corruption in transactions
  • Long-term national economic growth

The Core Problem

Despite abundant land and property, mortgages are scarce, interest rates are high, and banks avoid long-term housing finance. Government schemes underperform and corruption thrives.

Root Cause: Unmanaged title and mortgage risk.
