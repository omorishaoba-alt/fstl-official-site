Fannie Mae & Ginnie Mae Models

Blueprints to build Nigeria’s secondary mortgage liquidity

Home Fannie & Ginnie Mortgage Market Payment Options

Fannie Mae

Purchases loans from lenders, issues MBS, creating liquidity. A model for FMBN as a secondary purchaser and liquidity provider.

Ginnie Mae

Guarantees timely payments on MBS backed by government-insured loans—a template for guaranteeing low-income housing pools.

Implications

  • Legal reforms and standardisation are prerequisites
  • Title & mortgage insurance must be integrated to make pools investor-ready
  • Pension funds and insurers are natural anchor investors
