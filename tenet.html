FSTL Foundational Tenet

Primary Tenet

“No Country can rise above the sanctity, security, and stability of its Landed Property Rights.”

Option B – Expanded Interpretation

The success of any nation is tied directly to the certainty and reliability of its land ownership system. When titles are secure, investments rise. When ownership is protected, industries grow. When land is stabilised, peace, prosperity, and national progress become inevitable.
