Overview

FSTL leverages blockchain technology to secure land records, prevent tampering, and ensure immutable ownership history, improving investor confidence.

Benefits

  • Eliminates fraudulent title claims and double sales.
  • Enables real-time verification for banks, insurers, and government agencies.
  • Facilitates efficient secondary market transactions.
  • Supports national cadastral system modernization and urban planning.

FSTL Implementation

  • Digitizes all verified land titles using blockchain ledger.
  • Integrates with mortgage and title insurance products to reduce financial risk.
  • Provides secure portals for stakeholders to confirm property ownership instantly.
