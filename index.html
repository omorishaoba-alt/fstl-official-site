Legal Precedent

Foundational Case: Joshua Ogunleye v. Babatayo Oni – Established principles for adjudicating property disputes and validating titles.

  • Olumide v. Adeyemi (Appellate Court)
  • Okoro v. Uba (Supreme Court)
  • Adebayo v. Ogun (Appellate Court)
  • Emmanuel v. Nwosu (Supreme Court)

Observations: Judiciary inconsistencies exist; bureaucratic delays are prevalent. Title Guarantee mitigates these risks and reduces credit risk for financial institutions.

Title Guarantee & Insurance

Definition: Legally enforceable confirmation of property ownership and guarantee.

Benefits:

  • Security for buyers and sellers.
  • Reduces lender risk.
  • Enables collateralisation for capital market participation.
  • Supports FSTL’s first 50K product and knowledge-based ecosystem.

Strategic Use: Backup solution to escape bureaucratic delays. Enhances Nigeria land law compliance and enables structured investment across sectors.

Mortgage Insurance & Asset-Backed Securities (ABS)

Mortgage Insurance reduces lender risk and ensures developer credibility. ABS securitises property assets, providing liquidity for real estate, agriculture, and infrastructure projects.

Outcome: Integrated risk management ecosystem that benefits all stakeholders and enables participation in municipal bonds and capital markets.
