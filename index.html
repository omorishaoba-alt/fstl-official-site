About First Standard Title Limited (FSTL)

First Standard Title Limited (FSTL) is Nigeria’s independent authority providing title verification, mortgage readiness, and advisory services for land and landed property. Our mission is to protect investments, reduce transaction risk, and enhance financial confidence for landowners, investors, and institutions.

FSTL is pioneering the initiative to establish NATICON, the National Title Insurance Company of Nigeria, creating a trusted platform for secure land investments nationwide.

  • Peace of Mind: Ensure your land ownership is legally secure before purchase.
  • Time & Cost Savings: Avoid disputes and lengthy mortgage delays, saving potentially millions.
  • Financial Confidence: Lenders and investors trust FSTL-certified property, enabling faster deals.
  • Market Recognition: FSTL endorsement increases property credibility in the market.
