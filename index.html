First Standard Title Limited (FSTL)

Nigeria’s Pioneer Title Risk Intelligence & Mortgage-Readiness Authority

First Standard Title Limited (FSTL) provides professional title risk assessment and mortgage-readiness certification for land and landed property in Nigeria.

We support individual buyers, property developers, and financial institutions by identifying legal, documentary, historical, and transactional risks before purchase, financing, or development.

Core Professional Services

TRAR™ – Title Risk Assessment Report

The Title Risk Assessment Report (TRAR™) is a structured professional assessment that identifies legal defects, competing interests, documentation gaps, regulatory exposure, and transaction risks attached to a property title.

MRTC™ – Mortgage Readiness & Title Cleanliness Certification

The Mortgage Readiness & Title Cleanliness Certification (MRTC™) confirms that a property title meets the legal and risk standards required for mortgage financing, institutional investment, or structured development.

Pricing is determined by title complexity and risk exposure, not by property size.

NATICON – National Title Insurance Company of Nigeria

NATICON (National Title Insurance Company of Nigeria) is a national-scale title insurance framework being pioneered by First Standard Title Limited (FSTL).

NATICON is currently awaiting formal approval by the Federal Government of Nigeria. The framework is being developed based on FSTL’s live title risk operations, market intelligence, and institutional experience.
