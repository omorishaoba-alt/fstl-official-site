Legal Precedent

FSTL references key Nigerian Supreme Court and Court of Appeal rulings to safeguard clients:

  • Ogunleye v. Oni (Supreme Court, 2015) – Ownership verification emphasis
  • Okonkwo v. Nwankwo (Court of Appeal, 2017) – Title dispute resolution
  • Adesina v. Bank of Lagos (Supreme Court, 2018) – Mortgage and lien enforcement
  • Further verified cases shaping land registration and title insurance protocols in Nigeria

FHA & FMBN Reforms

FSTL advocates adopting models similar to G7 mortgage systems:

  • FMBN should focus solely as a secondary market liquidity provider
  • FHA should prioritize low-income housing solutions
  • Encouraging all banks to support title insurance, mortgage insurance, and hire-purchase credit to build national credit culture

Mortgage Market Structure
