Welcome to FSTL

FSTL is Nigeria’s pioneering institution securing land titles, enabling mortgage confidence, and transforming landed property into bankable capital.

Our Mission: To eliminate property risk, unlock real estate capital, and create trust infrastructure for Nigeria’s economy.

About FSTL

FSTL addresses systemic failures in land title verification, property risk, and mortgage insecurity in Nigeria. We operate at the intersection of law, insurance, technology, and capital markets — creating a new asset-trust layer for real estate.

Our Core Services

  • Title Insurance
  • Mortgage Insurance
  • Property Due Diligence & Risk Rating
  • Land Title Intelligence Systems
  • Capital Readiness for Landed Assets

Venture Capital Memorandum

1. Investment Summary

FSTL is raising strategic growth capital to scale Nigeria’s first institutional title insurance and mortgage risk infrastructure.

2. The Problem

Over 80% of land in Nigeria is unbankable due to defective titles, uncertainty, fraud, and legal opacity — trapping trillions in dead capital.

3. Our Solution

FSTL provides legally-backed title insurance, risk analytics, and verification systems that transform land into trusted financial assets.

4. Market Opportunity

Nigeria’s real estate market exceeds ₦200 trillion in value, yet less than 5% is mortgage-enabled. FSTL unlocks this gap.

5. Competitive Advantage

  • First-mover advantage in title insurance
  • Legal + Insurance + Technology convergence
  • Institutional trust positioning
  • Regulatory-aligned framework

6. Revenue Model

  • Insurance premiums
  • Title verification fees
  • Institutional partnerships
  • Platform licensing

7. Capital Use

Funds will be deployed into technology infrastructure, regulatory scaling, institutional partnerships, and national expansion.

8. Exit Vision

Strategic acquisition, infrastructure integration, or financial market listing as Nigeria’s trusted property-risk backbone.
