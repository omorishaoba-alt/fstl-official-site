Legal Precedent

FSTL’s legal backbone ensures secure property transactions and investor confidence.

Key Statutes

  • Land Use Act 1978 – land ownership, Governor’s Consent, occupancy rights
  • Property & Conveyancing Law – assignments, leases, conveyancing
  • Registration of Titles – title perfection and priority rules
  • Evidence Act & Contract Law – validity of agreements

Major Case Law

  • Ogunleye v Oni (1990) – Cannot transfer better title than possessed
  • Osho v Foreign Finance Corp (1991) – Registered title takes priority
  • Savannah Bank v Ajilo (1989) – Governor’s Consent is mandatory
  • Adeniran v Alao (2002) – Possession does not override documented title
  • Idundun v Okumagba (1976) – Five methods of proving title
