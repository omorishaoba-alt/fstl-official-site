Venture Capital Memorandum

1. Investment Summary

FSTL is raising Stage One venture capital to scale Nigeria’s first institutional title insurance and mortgage risk infrastructure.

2. The Problem

Over 80% of land in Nigeria is unbankable due to defective titles, fraud, litigation risk, and legal opacity — trapping trillions in dead capital.

3. The Solution

FSTL provides legally backed title insurance, verification systems, and risk analytics that transform land into trusted financial assets.

4. Market Opportunity

Nigeria’s real estate value exceeds ₦200 trillion, yet mortgage penetration remains below 5%. FSTL unlocks this gap.

5. Revenue Model

  • Insurance premiums
  • Verification fees
  • Institutional partnerships
  • Platform licensing

6. Capital Use

Technology infrastructure, regulatory scaling, partnerships, and national expansion.

7. Exit Vision

Strategic acquisition, infrastructure integration, or capital market listing.
