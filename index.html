About First Standard Title Limited (FSTL)

First Standard Title Limited (FSTL) is Nigeria’s pioneer in title insurance, mortgage insurance, and secure landed property rights (SLPR). Our mission is to safeguard property owners, prevent fraudulent claims, and create a reliable system for collateralisation that fuels economic growth. Through rigorous verification, legal guidance, and innovative private cadastral solutions, FSTL sets the standard for secure property asset management.
