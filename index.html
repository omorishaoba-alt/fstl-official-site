About First Standard Title Limited (FSTL)

First Standard Title Limited (FSTL) is Nigeria’s pioneering title risk and mortgage readiness authority for land and landed property.

FSTL exists to solve one problem: unsafe, disputed, and non-bankable land titles.

The Value FSTL Delivers

  • Prevents loss of capital from defective land titles
  • Reduces litigation, disputes, and government revocations
  • Makes land mortgage-ready and bankable
  • Accelerates property transactions and access to funding
  • Creates institutional trust in land investments

Revenue-Generating Services

Title Risk Assessment Report (TRAR™)

  • Independent legal and registry verification of land titles
  • Identification of encumbrances, defects, and risks
  • Actionable recommendations before purchase
  • Direct value: prevents costly disputes and loss

Mortgage Readiness & Title Cleanliness Certification (MRTC™)

  • Certifies land as mortgage-ready
  • Reduces lender due diligence time
  • Improves access to capital
  • Direct value: faster approvals, higher confidence

Strategic Project

NATICON

FSTL is pioneering and driving the establishment of NATICON — the National Title Insurance Company of Nigeria.

  • National platform for title insurance
  • Risk pooling and property confidence framework
  • Foundation for large-scale mortgage growth
