About FSTL

First Standard Title Limited (FSTL) is committed to safeguarding and securitising landed property assets in Nigeria, ensuring legal certainty, financial leverage, and economic empowerment for our clients.

Secure Landed Property Rights (SLPR)

FSTL offers solutions that secure landed property rights to prevent disputes, protect investments, and provide collateral for capital generation. Our expertise ensures clients can leverage their property assets effectively and legally.

Bureaucratic Viscosity & Landed Property Disputes

Nigeria’s bureaucratic inefficiencies often exacerbate property disputes. Our team highlights the systemic issues, streamlines property verification, and provides clients with reliable procedures to navigate complex administrative hurdles.

Judiciary and Governance

FSTL addresses the misuse of power in property disputes, including:

  • Improper use of police and enforcement agencies in property conflicts.
  • Judicial delays and corrupt practices affecting land cases.
  • Influence and failures of traditional rulers impacting dispute resolution.

We provide factual legal precedents and structured processes to ensure clients’ rights are protected.
