About FSTL

First Standard Title Limited (FSTL) is an independent authority focused on reducing legal uncertainty in property transactions and enabling confidence in real estate finance across Nigeria.

We provide deployable instruments that diagnose title risk, certify mortgage readiness, and support institutional reform in land administration.

Our Products

1. Title Risk Assessment Report (TRAR™)

A professional, independent evaluation of the legal and transactional risks attached to any property title in Nigeria.

What the report covers:
  • Title ownership verification
  • Registry searches and inconsistencies
  • Existing or potential litigation
  • Encumbrances, liens, and overriding interests
  • Government acquisition or revocation risks
  • Historical defects and red flags
  • Overall Risk Rating (Low / Medium / High)
Professional Decision Opinion:
  • Proceed
  • Proceed with Conditions
  • Do Not Proceed
Use Case: Buyers, developers, diaspora investors, law firms, and banks use TRAR™ to make informed property decisions.

2. Mortgage Readiness & Title Cleanliness Certification (MRTC™)

Certification confirming that a property has been professionally vetted and meets baseline expectations for mortgage financing.

  • Reduces bank due diligence cost
  • Accelerates mortgage approvals
  • Improves lender confidence
Outcome: A mortgage-ready property backed by independent verification.
