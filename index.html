Executive Summary

Objective: FSTL safeguards landed property rights, creates collateralisation for economic growth, and develops intellectual property ensuring sustainable competitive advantage.

  • Legal framework for property acquisition and dispute resolution.
  • Financial instruments including Title Guarantee, Mortgage Insurance, and ABS.
  • Risk mitigation strategies for financial institutions and the public.
  • Land industrialisation as a catalyst for infrastructure and economic growth.

Key Strategy:

  • Integrate Title Guarantee to bypass bureaucratic delays.
  • Provide mortgage insurance to reduce lender risk.
  • Introduce ABS for liquidity and capital formation.
  • Build a knowledge-based repository of judicial precedents and industry insights.
  • Use cadastral systems and blockchain for secure property records.
  • Leverage capital markets and municipal bonds to fund infrastructure.
