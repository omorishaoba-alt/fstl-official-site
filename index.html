About First Standard Title Limited

First Standard Title Limited (FSTL) is the pioneer of title insurance and mortgage insurance in Nigeria. We provide professional guidance and solutions to secure landed property rights and prevent bureaucratic misuse or abuse in property disputes.

Our work is backed by historical legal precedents, including cases from the Court of Appeal and the Supreme Court, to ensure robust protection of your landed assets.
