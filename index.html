About FSTL

First Standard Title Limited (FSTL) is Nigeria’s leading authority in land title verification and mortgage readiness certification. We protect investments, reduce legal risk, and provide confidence to landowners, investors, and institutions nationwide.

FSTL is driving the creation of NATICON, the National Title Insurance Company of Nigeria.

Our Services

Title Risk Assessment Report (TRAR™)

  • Identify legal and regulatory risks.
  • Prevent disputes and financial loss.

Mortgage Readiness & Title Cleanliness Certification (MRTC™)

  • Accelerates mortgage approvals.
  • Reduces lender due diligence time.
