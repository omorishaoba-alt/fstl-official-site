About FSTL

First Standard Title Limited (FSTL) is Nigeria’s foremost independent authority on title risk assessment and mortgage readiness. We exist to reduce uncertainty in property transactions and enable confidence in real estate finance.

Our Products

1. Title Risk Assessment Report (TRAR™)

The FSTL Title Risk Assessment Report (TRAR™) is a professional, independent evaluation of the legal and transactional risks attached to any property title in Nigeria.

What the report covers:
  • Title ownership verification
  • Registry searches and inconsistencies
  • Existing or potential litigation
  • Encumbrances, liens, and overriding interests
  • Government acquisition or revocation risks
  • Historical defects and red flags
  • Overall Risk Rating (Low / Medium / High)
Professional Decision Opinion:
  • Proceed
  • Proceed with Conditions
  • Do Not Proceed

2. Mortgage Readiness & Title Cleanliness Certification (MRTC™)

The Mortgage Readiness & Title Cleanliness Certification confirms that a property has been professionally vetted and meets baseline expectations for mortgage financing.

Certification confirms:
  • Legal vetting completed
  • No known material title defects
  • Mortgage risks identified and mitigated
  • Improved lender confidence
