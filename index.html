Knowledge Base

Title Guarantee

Title Guarantee provides legal assurance of ownership, as a backup and alternative to Title Insurance. It reduces bureaucratic delays, protects buyers, sellers, and financial institutions, and guarantees validity of title even in cases of FG bureaucratic inefficiencies.

Mortgage Insurance

Mortgage Insurance mitigates credit risk for banks, PMIs, FMBN, and other lenders, enabling safer land-backed lending for developers and individuals. It protects against default and financial loss.

Asset-Backed Securities (ABS)

Land-backed securities convert land assets into liquid financial instruments. ABS enables investors to access capital while supporting national infrastructure and development projects.

Land Industrialisation & Economic Development

  • Structured land use directly correlates with economic growth and development.
  • Absence of presidential/root title contributes to legal uncertainty and financial instability.
  • Sand-filling, dredging, and artificial islands create long-term constitutional and environmental risks.
  • FSTL Ecosystem: Investors → FSTL → Title Guarantee → Mortgage Insurance → ABS → Capital Market → Infrastructure → Economic Growth.
