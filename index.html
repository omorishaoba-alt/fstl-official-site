Executive Summary

Objective: FSTL safeguards landed property rights in Nigeria, creates collateralisation for economic growth, and develops intellectual property ensuring sustainable competitive advantage.

  • Legal framework for property acquisition and dispute resolution.
  • Financial instruments including Title Guarantee, Mortgage Insurance, and ABS.
  • Risk mitigation strategies for financial institutions and the public.
  • Land industrialisation as a catalyst for infrastructure and economic growth.
  • Blockchain-enabled cadastral registry for accuracy and transparency.

Key Strategy:

  • Integrate Title Guarantee as a backup to bypass bureaucratic delays.
  • Provide mortgage insurance to reduce lender risk.
  • Introduce ABS for liquidity and capital formation.
  • Build a knowledge-based repository of judicial precedents and industry insights.
  • Deploy FSTL cadastral on blockchain for immutable land verification.

Core Tenet

“No country can develop or become developed without the collateralisation of its landed property assets nor its securitisation of these assets to create capital and economic boom. Otherwise, it’s just wishful thinking.”

This tenet guides FSTL’s strategy: ensuring property rights are verifiable, securitised, and leveraged for economic development.

Legal Precedent

Foundational Case: Joshua Ogunleye v. Babatayo Oni – Established principles for adjudicating property disputes and validating titles.

  • Olumide v. Adeyemi (Appellate Court)
  • Okoro v. Uba (Supreme Court)
  • Adebayo v. Ogun (Appellate Court)
  • Emmanuel v. Nwosu (Supreme Court)

Observations: Judicial inconsistencies and bureaucratic delays are mitigated by FSTL’s Title Guarantee and knowledge repository.

Title Guarantee & Insurance

Definition: Legally enforceable confirmation of property ownership, also called Title Guarantee.

Benefits:

  • Security for buyers and sellers.
  • Reduces lender risk.
  • Enables collateralisation for capital market participation.
  • Alternative to bureaucratic delays in FG title issuance.

Integration with FSTL Products: Supports the first 50K knowledge product and risk management ecosystem.

Mortgage Insurance & Asset-Backed Securities (ABS)

Mortgage Insurance reduces lender risk; ABS securitises property assets for liquidity and capital formation.

Outcome: Integrated risk management and capital formation ecosystem supporting developers, lenders, and the public.

Land Industrialisation & Blockchain

Collateralised land fuels infrastructure, industrialisation, and economic growth. FSTL’s blockchain cadastral ensures transparency and immutability.

  1. FSTL as central knowledge repository.
  2. Developers acquire verified land titles.
  3. Financial institutions lend safely.
  4. ABS and capital markets fund projects.
  5. Government benefits from orderly land use and risk reduction.

Timebomb: Poorly planned sand filling and artificial islands create constitutional and environmental risks, highlighting the need for authoritative cadastral oversight.
