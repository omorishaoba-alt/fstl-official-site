Who We Are

First Standard Title Limited (FSTL) is a specialist firm dedicated exclusively to land and landed property. Our mandate is simple: remove uncertainty from land ownership and transactions.

How FSTL Works

  1. Submit Property Details – Client provides land information and selects the required report.
  2. Professional Title Review – FSTL conducts legal, registry, and historical title analysis.
  3. Risk Intelligence Delivered – Clear findings, grading, and recommendations are issued.
  4. Next-Step Advisory – Optional guidance for mortgage, investment, or risk mitigation.

The Value FSTL Adds

  • Prevents financial loss from defective or disputed land titles
  • Transforms land into mortgage-ready and investment-grade assets
  • Reduces litigation and post-transaction disputes
  • Shortens lender and investor decision timelines
  • Provides the professional foundation for national title insurance
