Legal Precedent

FSTL relies on Nigerian statutes, Supreme Court and Court of Appeal precedents for authoritative guidance:

  • Land Use Act 1978 – Land ownership, Governor’s Consent, occupancy rights
  • Property & Conveyancing Law – Assignment, lease, and conveyancing rules
  • Registration of Titles Acts – Controls title perfection and priority
  • Evidence & Contract Law – Validity of documents and agreements

Major Court Cases

  • Ogunleye v Oni (1990) – Cannot transfer better title than possessed
  • Osho v Foreign Finance Corp (1991) – Registered title priority
  • Savannah Bank v Ajilo (1989) – Governor’s Consent mandatory
  • Adeniran v Alao (2002) – Possession cannot override documented title
  • Idundun v Okumagba (1976) – Five methods of proving title
