Strategic Holding & Governance

Providing governance, capital structuring, and operational oversight for regulated property-risk subsidiaries in Nigeria.

Operating Subsidiaries

FSTL Holdings operates through its fully controlled subsidiaries:

1. First Standard Title Insurance Limited (FSTL) – delivering title and mortgage insurance solutions designed to enhance certainty, transparency, and risk protection in property transactions.

2. The National Title Insurance Company of Nigeria (NATICON) – providing complementary title insurance solutions, supporting national property transaction security and risk mitigation.

Core Functions

Title Insurance

Protection against ownership disputes, fraud, undisclosed encumbrances, and historical title defects.

Mortgage Insurance

Risk protection supporting lenders, borrowers, and structured mortgage transactions.

Risk Assessment & Advisory

Due diligence, title verification, and legal risk evaluation for property transactions.
