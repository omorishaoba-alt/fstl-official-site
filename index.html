Our Mandate

First Standard Title Limited (FSTL) exists to eliminate uncertainty, fraud, and inefficiency in property and transactional documentation. We provide a neutral, technology-driven infrastructure that enables professionals, institutions, and the public to transact with confidence.

What We Do

  • Title intelligence and document assurance
  • Transaction risk analysis and verification
  • Commission-based legal and agent distribution framework
  • Instant confirmation-based remuneration infrastructure
  • Capital-markets-ready documentation and data architecture

Commission-Based Partner Model

Lawyers and transaction agents operate as commission-based sales and execution partners. Upon confirmation of valid work:

  • Payments are automated and instant
  • No discretionary delays
  • Clear contractual terms embedded in the platform
  • Fair and generous commission structure designed for volume and sustainability

This model ensures trust, removes disputes, and supports high-throughput revenue generation without regulatory conflict.

Technology & Compliance

FSTL operates with or without government adoption. Our framework is designed to function independently while remaining compliant with existing legal and commercial principles.

Artificial intelligence is used strictly as infrastructure, analysis, and verification support — not as a substitute for licensed professionals.
