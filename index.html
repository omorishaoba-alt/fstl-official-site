About FSTL

With over 20 years of pioneering experience, First Standard Title Limited (FSTL) has been at the forefront of ensuring secure landed property rights (SLPR) in Nigeria. We provide innovative solutions that protect buyers, sellers, and financial institutions while enabling capital creation through the collateralisation of land assets.

Title Insurance

Purpose: Protects all stakeholders from property disputes and ensures smooth transactions.

Benefits to Buyers

  • Assurance of legal ownership
  • Protection against fraud
  • Confidence in property transactions

Benefits to Sellers

  • Smoother transfer of property
  • Reduced liability in disputes

Benefits to Financial Institutions

  • Reduced risk in mortgage lending
  • Enhanced security of collateral

Mortgage-Backed Securities (MBS)

FSTL products support the creation of Mortgage-Backed Securities, enabling investors to participate in securitized land assets. Our solutions integrate title insurance and ensure secure, compliant investments.

Upcoming financial products: securitisation of property, lending facilities, and investor-ready instruments.

Regulatory & Legal Framework

Our services align with the Nigeria Land Use Act, Wills Act, and other relevant laws. FSTL strengthens enforcement and transparency in land transactions while minimizing disputes in courts.
