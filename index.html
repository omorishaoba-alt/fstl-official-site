About FSTL

FSTL has over 20 years of experience as the pioneer of title insurance and mortgage insurance in Nigeria. We ensure that landed property rights are fully secured, creating a foundation for collateralisation, capital generation, and economic growth.

Our Services

Title Insurance and Land Law Compliance

FSTL ensures that all property transactions comply with Nigerian land laws, including the Land Use Act, Wills Act, and other relevant statutes. Our title insurance protects clients against fraudulent or disputed ownership, giving confidence to buyers, sellers, and lenders alike.

Mortgage-Backed Securities (MBS)

We facilitate the creation of asset-backed financial instruments from landed properties. Our expertise ensures that investors and financial institutions can securely engage in mortgage-backed securities, unlocking capital for economic growth.
