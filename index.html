Legal Precedent

Our legal backbone ensures risk-free property transactions:

  • Land Use Act 1978 – Basis of land ownership and Governor’s Consent
  • Property & Conveyancing Law – Governs assignments and leases
  • Major Cases: Ogunleye v Oni (1990), Osho v Foreign Finance Corp (1991), Savannah Bank v Ajilo (1989), Adeniran v Alao (2002), Idundun v Okumagba (1976)

These form the foundation for our Title Guarantee and risk mitigation products.

Housing Deficit & Statistics

Nigeria currently faces an estimated 17 million housing deficit (2025). Key factors include:

  • Poor land administration
  • Inefficient mortgage access
  • Limited investor confidence

FHA & FMBN Inefficiencies

  • Delayed approvals and disbursements
  • Limited portfolio coverage for middle- and low-income housing
  • Structural inefficiencies hindering land industrialisation
  • Contrast: G7 housing finance models show faster delivery, higher transparency, and investor participation
