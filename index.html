About FSTL

FSTL is Nigeria’s pioneering title insurance and mortgage insurance firm, with over 20 years of experience safeguarding landed property rights and enabling property-backed financial solutions.

We provide comprehensive services to ensure properties are legally verified, risk-assessed, and fully usable for financial purposes. Our expertise allows both individuals and institutions to harness the true economic potential of landed assets, reducing bureaucratic friction and litigation risk.

Our Mission

  • Secure and guarantee legitimate property ownership for clients.
  • Reduce financial and legal risk in property transactions.
  • Enable property collateralisation and securitisation to drive economic development.
  • Provide authoritative guidance on Title Guarantee, Mortgage Insurance, and Asset-Backed Securities (ABS).

Products & Services

1. Title Guarantee

  • Full verification of property ownership.
  • Issuance of Title Guarantee Certificate as authoritative legal assurance.
  • Protection against fraudulent claims, disputes, and bureaucratic delays.

2. Mortgage Insurance Readiness

  • Evaluation of properties for mortgage-backed loans.
  • Reduction of exposure for banks, PMIs, and financial institutions.
  • Prepares properties for financial leveraging in compliance with Nigerian law.

3. Asset-Backed Securities (ABS) & Mortgage-Backed Securities (MBS)

  • Assessment of properties for inclusion in ABS/MBS structures.
  • Conversion of property into liquidity for investment, development, and economic expansion.
  • Alignment with Nigeria’s capital market standards to enhance transparency and investor confidence.

4. Financial Risk Assessment

  • Comprehensive reports on credit risk associated with property transactions.
  • Assessment of risk to developers, financial institutions, PMIs, FMBN, and government stakeholders.
  • Supports informed decisions in property acquisition, lending, and development.

Legal Precedents

FSTL relies on established Nigerian legal precedents to safeguard client interests. Key cases include:

  • Joshua Ogunleye v. Babatayo Oni – foundational property dispute resolution.
  • Other Appeal Court & Supreme Court precedents – enforceability of title guarantees, risk mitigation, and protection against fraudulent claims.

Land Industrialisation & Economic Growth

Properly secured and collateralised property assets are central to economic expansion. Title Guarantee enables:

  • Real estate-backed capital formation
  • Municipal bonds and infrastructure financing
  • Identification of risks associated with unverified or artificially created land (sand filling, dredging, and make-believe islands)
