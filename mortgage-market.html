Market Anatomy

The mortgage market requires coordinated primary lenders (banks, PMBs), credit enhancers (title & mortgage insurance), and a robust secondary market (MBS/ABS) to recycle capital.

Key Bottlenecks

  • Insufficient long-term funding
  • Title insecurity and documentation gaps
  • High cost of construction and interest rates
  • Weak investor-ready mortgage pools
