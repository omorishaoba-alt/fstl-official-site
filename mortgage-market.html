Overview

Nigeria’s mortgage market remains nascent, representing less than 3% of GDP. The market is split into:

  • Primary Mortgage Market – banks, mortgage institutions, and FMBN intermediaries directly lending to homeowners.
  • Secondary Mortgage Market – FMBN and securitisation channels providing liquidity and risk mitigation.
  • Private Mortgage Insurance and Title Guarantee Providers – enhancing creditworthiness and security.

Market Challenges

  • Limited mortgage penetration due to high property costs and low disposable incomes.
  • Fragmented funding sources; over-reliance on government allocations and foreign loans.
  • Low awareness and adoption of title insurance and mortgage insurance products.
  • Weak regulatory enforcement and cadastral inefficiencies leading to disputed titles.

FSTL’s Strategic Role

FSTL bridges the gap by providing:

  • Title verification and guarantee services.
  • Mortgage insurance for banks and financial institutions.
  • Structured hire-purchase and financing solutions.
  • Support for securitisation of mortgages to attract long-term capital.
