FSTL OSHOBE

Land & Property Operations Interface

FSTL OSHOBE is the operational interface of First Standard Title Limited for recording and reviewing land and property matters.

Agent Fee Applies
All independently sourced land or property matters submitted through this platform are subject to an agent fee of 10%, payable upon successful review and engagement by FSTL.

Why OSHOBE?
OSHOBE (the deliverer) reduces your workload by:
- Automatically recording submissions
- Showing status of all matters (Pending / Reviewed / Delivered)
- Timestamping entries for clarity
- Organizing opportunities efficiently
Agents can focus on fieldwork while the app carries the administrative tasks.
