Benchmarks

  • High mortgage penetration (USA/UK/Canada) supported by robust secondary markets (Fannie/Freddie, covered bonds)
  • Government-backed guarantees for social housing (Ginnie Mae in the US)
  • Strong title systems and digital registries enabling bankability

Adaptation

Nigeria can adapt these lessons by building secondary market capacity, enforcing title security and using municipal/infrastructure bonds to fund urban projects.
