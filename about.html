ABOUT FIRST STANDARD TITLE LIMITED (FSTL)

Securing Landed Property Rights, Enabling Economic Growth.

OUR IDENTITY

First Standard Title Limited (FSTL) is the pioneer and custodian of Title Insurance, Mortgage Insurance, and Land Industrialisation in Nigeria. Our mission is to eliminate land fraud, unlock liquidity, and empower individuals, corporations, and governments with absolute confidence in land ownership.

OUR PURPOSE

FSTL exists to stabilise Nigeria’s land economy by providing certainty where historically there has been doubt. Through rigorous verification, secure architecture, and global‑standard insurance systems, we deliver a foundation upon which wealth, legacy, and national prosperity can be built.

OUR PHILOSOPHY

Land is power. Land is stability. Land is the foundation of civilisation. FSTL’s mandate is to protect that foundation fearlessly and professionally.

OUR COMMITMENT

  • Absolute integrity in all land and title operations.
  • Technology‑driven transparency for all parties.
  • Insurance‑backed security for every verified title.
  • National‑scale infrastructure for land industrialisation.
